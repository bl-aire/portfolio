Open-source Contribution

Outreachy Internship - JupyterHub: During Outreachy's 2022 Winter cohort, I contributed to Improving the Accessibility of JupyterHub. With the guidance of my mentors, the members of the community and Jupyter Lab's Accessibility team, I:

  • Evaluated JupyterHub’s pages using WAVE accessibility testing tool and axe DevTools.
  • Proposed solutions to accessibility issues pointed out by tests and discussed these solutions with members of JupyterHub’s community and the JupyterLab accessibility team.
  • Implemented changes approved by the team and evaluated the pages using screen readers like VoiceOver and ChromeVox to have a feel of what people with visual disabilities experience and see how efficient changes that were made are.
  • Automated the process of accessibility testing using Playwright so that accessibility defects are noted and corrected in the development phase.
